Analyse forex backtests with production-ready metrics

Compute Sharpe, Sortino, CAGR, max drawdown, profit factor, win rate and run Monte Carlo robustness checks — all in JavaScript. Built as a single-page app with a simple tabbed UI.

Interactive analytics, equity curves and trade tables

Key features

Performance metrics

Sharpe, Sortino, CAGR, total return, volatility and more — computed from trade/returns series.

Drawdown & risk

Max drawdown, drawdown tables and recovery periods — designed for robustness checks.

Monte Carlo

Fast Monte Carlo sampling for uncertainty and ruin probabilities — WebWorker-ready.

Single-page UX

Tabbed interface that feels like multiple pages while keeping the app a single bundle — no reloads.
